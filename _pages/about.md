### Research Interests
My research focuses on Adversarial Machine Learning, with particular emphasis on three key areas: enhancing the adversarial robustness of multi-modal foundation models, investigating the transferability of adversarial attacks across different machine learning architectures, and mitigating spurious correlations through training methodologies that leverage plausible counterfactuals.
